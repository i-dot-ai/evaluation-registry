Before you start

You can share an evaluation that is:

  • A planned evaluation
  • A ongoing evaluation
  • A complete evaluation report
Please be aware that all information added to the evaluation registry may be subject to a freedom of information request.

Before you start you'll need:

  • The title of the evaluation
  • All organisations involved in the evaluation
  • Type of evaluations
  • A link where the evaluation is published
